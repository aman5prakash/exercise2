Best Episodes

The One on the Last Night

Long before Pitch Perfect came along, Cups was a card game that made no sense. This is also the episode you played every single last night of college as you packed.

The One with Blackout

I'm trapped in an ATM vestibule with Jill GOODACRE.

The One With Ross's Tan

"I went to that tanning place your wife suggested." "Was that place the sun?!"

The One Where They're Up All Night

There is a Friends episode for literally everything that ever happens to you in your 20s and 30s. This is the one for when your fire alarm won't shut up, even when you throw it down the garbage chute in a blanket that says "property of Phoebe Buffay not Monica."

The One Where Everybody Finds Out

There's really nothing more to say besides, "They don't know we know they know we know!"

The One With All the Cheesecakes

I would do this for my nana's cheesecake. Sorry not sorry.

The One With All the Resolutions

What is your new year's resolution? Is it not to gossip? To not make fun of your friends? Or is it to try something new every day, like wearing a pair of leather paste pants?

The One With the Embryos

This episode is named after Phoebe getting pregnant with her brother's babies, but the trivia bet is its main highlight. You have probably won half the Friends trivia nights you've attended thanks to this episode. And you probably called your team "Miss Chanandaler Bong."

The One With the Apothecary Table

Fact: For the rest of your life, you will only be able to think of $51 as "one and fifty dollars."
